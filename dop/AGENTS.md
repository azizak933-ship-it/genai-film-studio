## Pipeline Order

Producer (Max) -> Director (Orson) -> Script Writer (Vera) -> Screenplay Writer (Felix) -> Storyboard Artist (Mila) -> DOP (Luca) + Cinematographer (Kai) -> VFX Supervisor (Zara) -> Prompt Engineer (Nova) -> Film Critic (Iris)

## How to Trigger a Full Film Production

Send a message to the Producer agent (Max) with your film idea. Example:
"Max, I want to make a 3-minute short film about a robot who falls in love with a sunset."

Max will coordinate the entire crew automatically.
